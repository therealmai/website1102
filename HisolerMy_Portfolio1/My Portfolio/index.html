HOBBIES

WHAT I LIKE TO DO

Swimming

I like to swim because it helps me relieve my stress. When I'm in the water, I tend to express my emotions by tiring myself out. It also gives me time to think about everything. My head engaged, fixed on what my body is doing. A physical mindfulness consumes my thoughts helping me escape from reality.

Calligraphy

Calligraphy has always been a platform for me to create beautiful art works through words and design. Although it is not as impressive as drawing or painting, it's very interesting for me to incorporate different designs into words. In a world dominated by typing and texting, calligraphy is becoming something of a relic. And yet, it remains unbelievably magical.

Poetry and Writing

With technology advancing, sitting down to write a letter, keeping a diary and taking notes can be considered activities which allows me to create a space of self-care. Ever since I was young, I have always been writing non-stop. I enjoy it so much since I've always had little to no difficulty in translating my thoughts to words. I also hold my pieces very dear to my heart.
